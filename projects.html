Spontaneity

Spontaniety is an App that aims to make quick event planning as seamless as possible. The app focuses on a simple UI to make events within a couple of hours. Users can have multiple groups with their friends and then specific events within those groups. However, the process of making events is much faster as it only requires a couple of clicks instead of scrolling and a lot of typing. This project was made with XCode and parse as a back-end framework.

AUV Robotics
