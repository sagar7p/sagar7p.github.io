What is a Code-A-thon

Our Code-A-Thon, often called Hackathons, is where a group of passionate developers and designers create something amazing in a limited time fram. This can range from websites, to iOS apps, to even Oculus Rift Games. We are giving students like you an opportunity to form learn something new, make something new, and showcase your product! It's always a ton of fun and we have so many people here to make your visions come true!

HackSC: High School Edition

This particular Code-A-Thon is a great way for high schoolers like yourself to get interested in what Computer Science can do. Our event will be from 10 am - 8 pm on USC's campus with lunch and dinner included. There will be many mentors around to help you guys with your products but you will be the one leading the project. We are really excited to see what you build!
